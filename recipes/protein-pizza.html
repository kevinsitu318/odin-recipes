Protein Pizza

Low calorie, satiating, delicious!

Do you want to lose weight AND eat delicious food??? Look no further because this is the ultimate weight loss recipe that you've been searching your whole life for! The recipe is as follows:

Ingredients

  • 100g fat free greek yogurt
  • 70g egg whites
  • 40g all purpose flour
  • 10g coconut flour
  • 7g baking powder
  • 1 tsp onion salt, italian seasoning, garlic powder
  • 28g fat free mozzarella cheese
  • 30g turkey pepperoni
  • 60g marinara sauce

Steps

  1. Combine yogurt and egg whites into a bowl and mix
  2. Add in both flours and baking powder and mix
  3. Season the batter with salt, italian seasoning, and garlic powder
  4. Line a baking sheet with wax paper and spray with non-stick spray
  5. Pour in batter and spread out into pizza shape
  6. Throw the baking sheet into a convection oven and bake for 8 minutes at 400 F
  7. After baking, remove the pizza and top with sauce, cheese and pepperoni
  8. Put the pizza back into the oven and bake for another 8 minutes
  9. Enjoy!
