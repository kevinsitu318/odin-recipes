Breakfast Burrito

Fast, low calorie, perfect in the morning!

Is there any more to explain? It's fast to prepare and fast to eat! It's also low calorie and perfect in the morning when you have limited time to cook and fills you up! This high protein, low calorie burrito will leave you wanting more! The recipe is as follows:

Ingredients

  • 90g lean turkey sausage
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 1 flour tortilla
  • 28g fat free mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the turkey sausage in a frying pan until cooked
  2. Remove the sausage from the pan for later
  3. Beat 2 whole eggs and pour into frying pan
  4. Cook the eggs for roughly 2 minutes on low and remove
  5. Heat the tortilla in the same pan for 1 minute on each side
  6. Remove the tortilla and add in sausage, eggs and cheese
  7. Wrap the tortilla into a burrito and seal the burrito in the pan
  8. Enjoy!!
